## ‚≠ê **Important Notes**

- **Accusative for Masculine**:
    
    - **Inanimate** nouns ‚Üí same as **Nominative**.
        
    - **Animate** nouns ‚Üí same as **Genitive**.
        
- **Neuter Accusative** ‚Üí always equals **Nominative**.
    
- Feminine nouns on **-—å** behave like other feminine nouns in all cases.
    
- Neuter nouns ending in **-–º—è** (like –≤—Ä–µ–º—è, –∏–º—è) have soft endings similar to **-–µ** nouns.
